# Squad: Intelligence

## Mission
Monitor external signals and surface relevant information.

## Agents

| Agent | Role | Trigger | Output |
|-------|------|---------|--------|
| market-scanner | Monitor industry news | Daily | briefs |
| competitor-tracker | Track competitor moves | Weekly | reports |
| trend-spotter | Identify emerging trends | Monthly | insights |

## Tools
- WebSearch
- WebFetch
- Firecrawl MCP

## Success Metrics
- Signal-to-noise ratio
- Time to insight
- Actionability of findings
